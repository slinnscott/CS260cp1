Today's Opening: The Caro - Kann Defense

1. e4 c6 2. d4 d5

The second variation of the Caro - Kann defense is known as the exchange varation where white chooses to trade pawns in the center of the board leading to this common position.

This opening leads to 3 major variations of play. The first of which is the advance variation, where white advances their pawn, 3. e5.

The third and last variation is known as the classical variation, where white simply continues their development and does not choose to exchange their pawns. This often leads to this postition.
